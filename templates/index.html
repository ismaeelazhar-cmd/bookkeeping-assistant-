Compound Journal Entry

For one payment or receipt that splits across several accounts (e.g. a BACS run covering Rent + Utilities + Insurance from one bank line). Pick the one account every line below shares — and add a line per other account. This posts as linked entries sharing one journal ID, with the exact same net ledger effect as a single multi-line posting.

Payroll Journal

Enter the figures from your payroll run (or payslip) — this posts DR Salary Expense against CR NI Payable / PAYE Payable / Pension Payable / Net Pay Payable in one linked journal. Net pay is calculated, not entered.

Dividend

Posts DR Retained Earnings against CR Dividends Payable (declared, not yet paid) or CR a bank account (paid immediately). Blocked if the amount exceeds distributable reserves (cumulative net profit, less dividends already posted) — posting an unlawful dividend has real consequences for directors.

Statement of Cash Flows

Built directly from cash movements in your ledger (IAS 7 direct method).

Comparative Period

This period's P&L vs. the same dates one year earlier (the From/To above shifted back exactly 365 days), with £ and % variance. Uses the same period set in Financial Statements above — Generate that first.

Cash Flow Forecast

Projected cash position: current cash, plus outstanding receivables/payables and recurring journal commitments due within each window. Not a guarantee — customers and suppliers don't always pay on the due date.

Tax Computation (estimate)

A working estimate only — uses the Financial Statements period above (Generate that first). Depreciation is added back (it isn't tax-deductible) and capital allowances are deducted instead; enter your own capital allowances figure since this app doesn't track AIA/WDA pools.

Self Assessment Helper (SA103)

For sole traders: maps P&L accounts to SA103 self-employment boxes by keyword, using the Financial Statements period above. A starting point for your return, not a substitute for checking each account lands in the right box.

Fund Accounting

For charities/nonprofits: tag transactions with a fund (restricted/designated/unrestricted) and get a Statement of Financial Activities. Off by default — turning it on changes nothing else about how this company works.

Cost Centres / Departments

For trading companies: tag transactions with a department (Sales, Operations, HR) and get a P&L broken down by department. Off by default — turning it on changes nothing else about how this company works.

AI settings (Claude API key)

Stored against this company on the server (in this app's database). The Claude API call happens server-side — this key is never sent to or readable from your browser, including by this app's own frontend. Powers Ask Your Ledger (Dashboard) and "Analyze with AI" (Bank → Import Bank Statement).

Clarification confidence threshold

AI suggestions scoring below this go to the Clarification Queue (Bank page) instead of being suggested automatically. Lower = the AI guesses more often; higher = more lines get held for your review.

Bank feed credentials (Plaid)

From a free account at dashboard.plaid.com. Secret is write-only — encrypted at rest, never sent back to this browser. Connect or sync the actual bank account under the Bank page once these are set.
